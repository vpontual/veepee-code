- `gemma4:26b` — was tried as a Llama Rider replacement on 2026-04-13/14 and failed. Expected to underperform qwen on the new gates (multi-turn consistency, regression REG-001 hallucination guard, exercises).

- If `gemma4:26b` actually wins overall, the new scorer is wrong or the OLD production failures were operational, not capability.

- If neither model emits any `tool_calls` during TEST_SUITE, the cold-start preload isn't enough and we need a stronger warm-up or a different approach for "tools-shy" models.
